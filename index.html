Greetings! I'm Sagar Jogale

A seasoned Unity game developer with nearly 5 years of hands-on experience crafting immersive experiences for Android and iOS mobile platforms. As a dedicated professional, I have not only honed my skills in game development but have also taken on leadership roles, serving as a team leader in my previous organization.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of contributing to a diverse array of projects, spanning the realms of 2D, 3D, and augmented reality (AR) gaming. One of my passions lies in the creation of educational games, where I've had the opportunity to build engaging and interactive learning experiences for kids from the ground up.

My expertise extends beyond the game mechanics themselves; I have delved into the intricacies of in-app purchases (IAP), seamlessly integrated Firebase Analytics for valuable insights, and strategically implemented Admob Ads to optimize monetization strategies.

Join me on a journey through my portfolio, where each project reflects not only my technical prowess but also my commitment to delivering innovative and impactful gaming experiences. I invite you to explore the fusion of creativity and technology that defines my approach to Unity game development.
